About Haleys Zoo

Why Choose Us?

  1. Haleys Zoo has decades of experience working with exotic animals, which means every aspect of your animal encounter is first class.
  2. Haleys Zoo are licenced to operate as a Zoo. This means they are inspected to the same standards as all UK Zoo's. This includes such large zoo's as Chester Zoo!
  3. There are many animal encounter companies throughout the UK. Some claim to offer a lot for a little price, however there are some important questions that you should be asking when deciding who to use.

Voluntary Work / Work Experience

  1. Volunteers play a very important role within Haleys Zoo as they assist our staff and provide excellent customer service, which in turn helps with the success of the business.
  2. Haleys Zoo welcomes volunteers to assist in our daily care and upkeep of our animals. We can also offer voluntary positions in marketing and business studies. We are a small family run business so you may find that you are asked to carry out duties that appear unrelated to the voluntary position.
  3. Volunteer support is vital as it allows us to carry out additional, but important tasks such as behavioural observations or creating new enrichment ideas.
