Welcome

Welcome to Stoke Warriors Hockey Club. We are a thriving and ever-expanding club located in North Staffordshire, based at Newcastle-under-Lyme School.

Stoke Warriors Hockey Club provides anyone of any age, ability or experience the opportunity to play hockey, and we are constantly looking to welcome new members.

The club runs a men's teams, a women's teams, and a junior team, providing opportunities for everyone to play competitive hockey at a level best suited to them.

Our clubhouse, the 45 Club, is located only minutes from the pitch, with social events held there or at other venues throughout the year.

Membership

Membership fees cover all league matches and pre-season friendlies but do not include any additional matches such as cup competitions or summer league games which will be charge on a game-by-game basis.

There is a 50% discount for goalkeepers who provide their own goalkeeping kit.

  • Seniors: £250 per annum
  • Juniors: £150 per annum
  • Students: £110 per annum

Club House

The clubhouse has recently undergone a complete refurbishment and can now be hired for external functions but is also used throughout the year for our celebrations and regular fund-raising and social events.

Top five moments of 2022-23 season

Last season was a great one for the club, despite the challenges of COVID and lockdown.

We celebrated some amazing achievements:

  1. Ladies team winners of Division 2 Ladies' league
  2. Men's team were runners-up in Division 2 Men's league
  3. Junior team promoted to Division 1 U16s league
  4. Refurbishment of the clubhouse
  5. Fund-raising reaches £1000 target
